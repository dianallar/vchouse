VCHouse.gov Terms of Service

1. Account Creation and Profile Requirements

Important Notice: VCHouse.gov is an extension project of the Virtual Congress Discord political simulator. By using this site, you affirm that you are a participating member of the Virtual Congress Discord Server.

1.1 Profile Creation Guidelines

  • Users must use their real roleplay name or a clearly identifiable pseudonym.
  • Profile pictures must be:
    • Professional headshots or portraits
    • Clear and recent photographs
    • Similar in style to official congressional portraits
    • Free of inappropriate content or political messaging
  • Biographical information should be factual and verifiable

2. District Claims and Representation

  • Users may only claim one district at a time
  • District claims must be approved by site moderators
  • Representatives must maintain active participation
  • Claims may be revoked for inactivity, ascension to other offices, resignation, or rule violations

3. Content and Behavior Guidelines

  • All content must be appropriate for a government website
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory content
  • No impersonation of other members of Virtual Congress
  • Maintain professional decorum in all interactions
  • No commercial advertising or spam

4. Profile Picture Requirements

  • File format: JPEG, JPG, PNG, or GIF
  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • Minimum resolution: 400x400 pixels
  • Must be a clear headshot with professional appearance
  • Background should be neutral and professional
  • No filters, effects, or heavy editing

5. Moderation and Enforcement

  • Site administrators reserve the right to:
    • Approve or reject district claims
    • Remove inappropriate content
    • Suspend or terminate accounts
    • Modify these terms as needed
  • Appeals can be made through the site's contact form

6. Privacy and Data Usage

  • Personal information will be protected
  • Profile information may be publicly visible
  • Usage data may be collected for site improvement
  • No data will be sold to third parties
